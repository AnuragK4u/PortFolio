UI/UX Design

If they are two separate roles, why are there job postings for UX / UI designers? Sometimes these are considered two separate fields — UX focusing on research and ideation and UI focusing on aesthetics and branding — but, they are both essential in product design. UX and UI designers work in tandem, and some companies may hire one person to complete both roles.
